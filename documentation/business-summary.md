# Business Summary

**Objectives:**  
- Provide a clear view of sales performance  
- Identify revenue trends and campaign effectiveness  
- Support commercial decision-making with accurate KPIs  

**Stakeholders**  
- Digital Echoes leadership  
- Sales and marketing teams  
- Finance and operations  

**Context**  
Digital Echoes needed a reliable, automated, and scalable way to understand sales performance across an 8 year period, campaigns, and customer segments. 
This project delivers that visibility using SQL, Power BI, and KPI modelling.
